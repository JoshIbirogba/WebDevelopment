About Us

As a company in environmental tech services, we partner with customers to provide sustainable solutions across their operations by bridging the gap between customers and recycling centers. Through our variety of products, services, and materials for almost every industry we help make a positive impact on people and the planet.

Our commitment to the community is unparalled and has yielded a lot of success over the past few years since the comapny was founded. We currrently have an active network of 2 million users across seven provinces in Canada. All of ths has been acheievable thanks to the amazing partnership we have had with the ten recycling companies we partner with.

Meet The Team

Joshua Ibirogba

CTO & Founder

Joshua Ibirogba is a Nigerian born software engineer who co-founded the company DePlast. He attended Grande Prairie Regional College(now Northwestern Polytechnic) where he studied computer systems technology. He then proceeded to get a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from the prestigious University of Toronto.

Joshua has been a leading voice in the tech industry since his days in school when he founded the comapny, alongside his friends he met in college. Joshua and his co-founders have grown the company valuation to over $300million. He is happily married to his wife, and they have two lovely kids.

David Wokoma

COO & Co-Founder

David Wokoma is a Nigerian born-Canadian physiotherapist, who co-founded the company DePlast alongside Joshua, Stephen and Kelvin. He attended Grande Prairie Regional College (now Northwestern Polytechnic) where he graduated with a diploma in Kinesiology before proceeding to the University of Alberta to complete his degree, where he finished sum cum laude.

David got his masters degrees in Environmental Science and Renewable Energy Management from University of Toronto and McGill University respectively. He is the Chief Operations Officer of Deplast. David is happily married to his wife, Dominica and they both have six amazing kids.

Kelvin Igwe

CEO & Co-Founder

Kelvin Igwe is a Nigerian born-Canadian Environmental and Renewable Energy expert. He attended the University of Manitoba, where emerged as the best undergraduate student in Environmental Science. He then proceeded to get his master's degree in Renewable Energy from the prestigious Harvard University in the United States.

Before joining DePlast, Kelvin has offered his expertise to the Government of Canada where he served as an adviser to the Prime Minister on Climate Change. Kelvin has authored and co-authored several books on climate change and the environment. He is happily single.
